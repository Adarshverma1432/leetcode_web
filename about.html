About CodeJourney 🚀

CodeJourney is not just a platform, it's a daily habit builder for developers. We focus on helping you master Data Structures and Algorithms through consistency, structured practice, and deep understanding.

Why This Platform? 💡

Many students start DSA but quit halfway due to lack of direction. CodeJourney solves this problem by providing a clear roadmap, categorized problems, and step-by-step solutions. The goal is not just solving questions, but understanding patterns.

Start from Level 1 problems and gradually move forward. Each problem comes with explanation, approach, and code. Practice daily, track your progress, and build strong logic step by step.
